## Headline

**MemPhant beats Syndai's knowledge stack on its own mined golden sets** at the deep-recall
operating point (modernbert, k=50, budget 8192): QA 0.367/0.400 vs Syndai 0.217/0.267 —
**pooled +0.142 [+0.058, +0.225]**, and each set individually CI-clean (+0.150 [+0.033,+0.250] v1;
+0.133 [+0.017,+0.267] v2). The pre-registered flip rule (pooled excl-0 + per-set sign agreement)
is satisfied with margin; the flip replicates without resource chunks (+0.100 [+0.008,+0.183]).
W10's HOLD is superseded at this config.

**The asterisk, stated plainly:** the winning config feeds the reader a median ~38.5k chars of
evidence per question vs Syndai's 2.8k (~14×). At Syndai-comparable volume (k=10/8192) we still
lose pooled (−0.100 [−0.175,−0.025]). Gold IS retrieved at depth but under-ranked in the top-10 —
the flip is real engine-vs-engine at each side's chosen operating point, but it buys accuracy with
reader-context volume (cost/latency), not retrieval precision. Replacement wiring (R6) is NOT
unlocked by this alone; the named next lever is rank compression (cross-encoder/fusion re-ordering
of the deep pool into small-k budgets — the W8 seam is the natural candidate).

## Diagnosis chain that forced the design (chronological, all pre-registered)

1. Breadcrumb hypothesis (from R0's control arm) FALSIFIED: retrieval up ~+0.017 both sets, QA
   didn't convert — the third retrieval-gain-no-conversion instance of the campaign.
2. Miss taxonomy (`.superpowers/sdd/r1-miss-taxonomy.md`): 17/17 Syndai-win/MemPhant-loss questions
   were gold-ABSENT retrieval misses (zero reader failures), dominated by wrong-section-within-
   right-file in long multi-topic docs; argued against lexical re-weighting and for finer granularity.
3. D1 exposed the fan-out effect (top-5 improves when k=50 is requested); D2 showed packing more
   converts (+0.092 excl0). Together: gold lives at rank 11–50; depth beats everything tried.
4. Chunking built on that evidence (rung-4 machinery extended to document resources, 43b8702,
   flag-gated; 468/3257 sections chunk → 1109 chunks) — helps v1 at depth, ns pooled.
